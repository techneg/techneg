Why Choose Techneg?

More about Techneg

TECHNEG is a multi-purpose technology training center located in the Upper West Region of Ghana. TECHNEG seeks to address the gap in providing skills for next generation workers for Africa..

Our Mission

To inspire and empower African Entrepreneurs to solve their immediate socio-economic challenges by leveraging technology,, resources and network collaboratively, doing well while doing so.

Our Vision

Our vision is to achieve the best Startup and Technology success stories in Africa.
