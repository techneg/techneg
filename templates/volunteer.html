How It Works

Volunteer With Us.

Are you willing to invest few hours to volunteer with us? Based on your skill set, we would match you to volunteering roles in our organization.

Volunteers will be working one-to-one and with small groups of ladies to offer them the needed support either on-site or remotely. Volunteers are also welcome to help out with fundraising, sales and marketing, recruitment and many others.

Apply

Be a Mentor.

We are happy to have industry experts mentor our ladies either face-to-face or remotely. We would particularly love to have people who can provide the support that would be relevant to their personal and professional development.

Based on availability, mentors can have sessions with ladies at least an hour per week to catch up with their progress. If you are interested in promoting our vision by being a mentor, kindly fill our mentorship application form.
